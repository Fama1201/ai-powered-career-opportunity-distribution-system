Our Mission

Jobify CVUT is an intelligent platform designed to bridge the gap between talented students from FIT ƒåVUT and relevant internship and job opportunities. We leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence to create meaningful connections that benefit both students and employers.

What We Do

Our platform uses the EXPERTS.AI platform for opportunity sourcing, OpenAI's GPT for intelligent CV analysis and personalized recommendations, and PostgreSQL for persistent profile and opportunity tracking. We transform the traditional job search process into an intelligent, AI-driven experience that matches students with opportunities that truly align with their skills and career aspirations.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI-powered career platform for university students, making job searching more efficient, personalized, and successful. We envision a future where every student finds their perfect career match through intelligent technology.

How It Works

For Students

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up and upload your CV. Our AI automatically extracts your skills, education, and experience.

2

AI Matching

Our intelligent system analyzes your profile and matches you with relevant job opportunities based on your skills and interests.

3

Get Recommendations

Receive personalized job recommendations with match scores. See why each opportunity is perfect for you.

4

Apply & Track

Apply to positions with one click and track your application status. Get AI-powered career advice along the way.

For HR Professionals

1

Register Your Company

Create an HR account and register your company. Set up your company profile and preferences.

2

Post Job Opportunities

Create detailed job postings with requirements, benefits, and deadlines. Our platform makes it easy to reach qualified candidates.

3

AI Candidate Ranking

Receive AI-powered candidate recommendations ranked by match quality. See the best candidates for your positions first.

4

Manage & Hire

Review candidate profiles, download CVs, and manage your hiring pipeline. Track applications and make data-driven decisions.
